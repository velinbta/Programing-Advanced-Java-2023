8. Custom Comparator
Write a custom comparator that sorts all even numbers before all odd ones in ascending order. Pass it to an Arrays.sort() function and print the result.

Examples:

Input 1:
1 2 3 4 5 6

Output 1:
2 4 6 1 3 5

Input 2:
-3 2

Output 2:
2 -3

Input 3:
-111 2 333 444 555 22 73 69 13

Output 3:
2 22 444 -111 13 69 73 333 555
