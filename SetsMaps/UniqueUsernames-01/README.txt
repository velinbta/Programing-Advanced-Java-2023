1. Unique Usernames
Write a simple program that reads a sequence of usernames from the console and keeps a collection with only the unique ones. Print the collection on the console in order of insertion:

Examples:

Input 1:
6
Hello
Hello
Hello
World
Hello
Greetings

Output 1:
Hello
World
Greetings

Input 2:
10
Peter
Maria
Peter
George
Stephen
Maria
Alexander
Peter
Stephen
George

Output 2:
Peter
Maria
George
Stephen
Alexander
